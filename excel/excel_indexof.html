In Excel, how do you check IndexOf (or Contains) of a string?

You can't sorry. Oh wait:

Excel "String Contains"

is:

=ISNUMBER(SEARCH("fred",B2))

Excel "IndexOf String"

is:

=SEARCH("fred",B2)
